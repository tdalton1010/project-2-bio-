BioTerrorist Attacks


Bioterrorism though not mentioned much has always been and continues to be a threat, especially with advances in science & technology.


Why do we care? This information is advantageous in preparedness planning and mitigation in the event of an attack.


Bioterrorism data was webscraped, cleaned, and organized into the dataset visualized here. Bioterrorism has been and continues to be a growing issue throughout the world. This map tells the story of previous attacks throughout the world and the impact it had on the population.
